SchoolChoice

Intelligent academic advising platform for Hong Kong secondary schools

What It Does

SchoolChoice gives school counsellors a single workspace to manage student academic data, evaluate university fit using real JUPAS admission formulas, and generate personalised academic plans — replacing spreadsheets, guesswork, and manual cross-referencing with an integrated, data-driven workflow.

Core Capabilities

Student Data Management

Complete academic profiles: HKDSE grades across multiple sittings (mock, trial, official), IELTS scores, teacher evaluations, extracurricular activities, awards. Supports CSV/Excel bulk import for class-level data entry.

University Matching

Computes admission probability for each JUPAS programme using the exact published scoring formula. Each programme has its own subject weightings, best-N selection rules, and grade scale. Results are calibrated against real published admission statistics (median, lower quartile).

Academic Plan Generation

Produces personalised HTML plan documents combining quantitative match data with AI-generated advisory language. Three template styles. Section-by-section editing. AI chat for refinements. Print-ready output.

Cohort Analytics

Class-level grade distributions, target school popularity, at-risk student identification. Filter by exam sitting to track improvement from mock to official. Bulk grade entry grid for efficient data management.

When a counsellor selects target universities for a student, the system runs the matching engine in real time. Each target school shows a colour-coded match percentage, eligibility status (pass/fail with specific failing criteria), and an at-risk flag when the student's predicted score falls below the programme's lower quartile of admitted students.

The matching engine uses programme-specific formulas published by each university — not a generic algorithm. A student applying to HKU Engineering is scored differently from the same student applying to CUHK Business, because the programmes weight subjects differently.

Multi-year grade tracking

Students typically sit mock exams in Form 5 and official HKDSE in Form 6. The system tracks grades across multiple sittings and years, computes predicted grades from the most recent data, and uses the best available scores for university matching. Counsellors can see grade progression over time and adjust advice accordingly.

Key design decision: The scoring engine is fully deterministic — no machine learning, no training data, no black-box models. Every score traces to a published university formula and verified admission statistics. AI is used only for generating advisory language in academic plans, where it adds value without affecting the accuracy of quantitative recommendations.

Multi-tenancy

Each school operates in its own isolated data space. A counsellor at School A cannot see students from School B. Isolation is enforced at the API layer through organisation-scoped JWT tokens — every database query is automatically filtered by the counsellor's organisation.

Privacy & Compliance

PDPO Compliance

Per-student consent tracking (data processing, AI analysis, export, sharing). Consent can be granted and revoked with full audit trail. Student names are anonymised before being sent to third-party AI providers.

Data Protection

PII blocklist prevents sensitive fields (medical notes, disciplinary records, SSN, date of birth) from being included in AI prompts. All AI output is sanitised for injection attacks. Passwords hashed with bcrypt. JWT tokens expire after 60 minutes.
